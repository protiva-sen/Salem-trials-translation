(Testimony of
Thomas Chandler
v.

)
[+ September 14, 1692]
The tistimony of
Thomas Chandler
aged about 65 who saith that I have often hard
Samuell wardle
of Andovr till yung person thire fortine and he was much adicted to that and mayd sport of it and farther said not
Jurat in Curia. (Reverse)
Thomas Chandler
depo agst
Sam'l Wardwell
