[++ September 9, 1710]
An Account of the Expences of
Nathaniel Dane
of Andover for his wife
Deliverance
who was accused of witchcraft and Suffered Imprisonment 13 weeks in the year 1692 And for his man Servant who was Imprisoned eight weeks upon the same account.
viz
For prison fees and money and provision
necessarily Expended
while they were in prison } 3 - 13 - 0
it money to the sheriffe &
the Clerk and  the keeper when
my wife was discharged upon Bail } 1 - 0 - 0

mr Barnard
to  give in this Acct  Pr
*Nathaniel Dane
