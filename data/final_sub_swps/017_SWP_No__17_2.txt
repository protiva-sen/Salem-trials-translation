SWP No. 17.2

(
Rev. Increase Mather's
Report of his Conversation in Prison with
Mary Bridges, Sr.
)
[October 19, 1692 ]
Goodwife Bridges
said that she had confessed against herself things which were all utterly false; and that she was brought to her confession by being told that she certainly was a witch, and so made to believe it, -- though she had no other grounds so to believe.
( Charles W. Upham, Salem Witchcraft. Boston, 1867, II, 406 .)
