(Order of
Lawrence Lacey
)
[January 7, 1712]
To the Gentlemen of the Committee appointed by the Governor & Council to distribute the money allowed by the Gen'll Court to such as were sufferers in the year 1692
Please to pay and deliver unto
Abraham Foster
of Andover the sum allowed unto me and his Receipt shall fully discharge you from the same.
