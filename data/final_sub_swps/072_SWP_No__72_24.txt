SWP No. 72.24

(Deposition of
Sarah Bibber
v.
Elizabeth How
)
[June 30, 1692]
the depozition of
sarah beber
aged 36 year testifieths and saith the day that
elizabeth how
was examnd I saw her hurt elizabeth hubbort  and
ann putnam
and abegel williams. and emediately. she fell apon me and Choked me and thru me down and hurt one of my leags very much. and
elizabeth how
did aflicte
mary wolcott
severall tims. and one the day of har examination --
Sarah Vibber
: owned to the Jury of inquest: the above written to be a true evidence of hers:: upon oath: June: 30th 1692
Jurat in Curia
(Witchcraft Papers, no. 22b, Massachusetts Historical Society, Boston, MA. )
