[May 25, 1692 ]
To the Keeper of their Majest's Goale in Boston
You are in theire Majest's names hereby required to take into  your care and safe Custody the Bodys of
Martha Cory
the wife of
Giles Cory
of Salem farmes husbandman
Rebecka Nurse
the wife of
francs Nurce
of Salem Village husbandman.
Dorothy Good
the  daugter of
Wm Good
afores'd husbandman.
Sarah Cloyce
the wife of
peter Cloyce
of Salem Village husbandman.
John procter
of Salem farmes husbandman and
Elizabeth
the wife of s d
John Procter
of Salem farmes Husbandman who all and every one of them stand charged in behalfe of theire Majest's for feloniously Committeing Sundry Acts of Witchcraft Lately, at Salem Village, on the bodys of
Ann putnam
the daugter of
Thomas putnam
Abigail Williams
Eliz. Hubbert
& others of Salem Village afores'd whereby great hurt hath beene donne to theire bodys Contrary to the peace of our Sov'r L'd  & Lady Wm & Mary of England &c King & Queen, whome you are all well to secure untill thay shall be delivered by due order of Law  And hereof you are not to faile
Dated Boston, May 25't 1692.
*John Hathorne
per vs
