SWP No. 69.8

(Deposition of
Margaret Knight
v.
Abigail Hobbs
)
[+ April 19, 1692]
The deposition of
margaritt knight
aged about 18 years who testifeith and saith that about ayear agoe
Abigail Hoobs
. and hir mother ware att my fathers house: and
Abigail Hoobs
said to me
Margaritt
are you baptized: and I said yes: then said she my mother is not baptized but said I will baptize hir and immediatly took watter and sprinckeled in hir mothers face and said she did #[say] baptized hir in the name of the ffather Son and Holy Ghost
(Reverse)
Ma. Knight
( Essex County Court Archives, Salem -- Witchcraft Vol. 1, no. 158, Massachusetts Supreme Judicial Court, Judicial Archives, Massachusetts State Archives, Boston, MA. )
