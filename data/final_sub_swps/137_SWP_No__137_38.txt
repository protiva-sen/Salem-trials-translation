SWP No. 137.38

( Testimony of
Abigail Williams
v.
John Willard
)
[+ May 18, 1692]
The Testimony of
Abigail Williams
witnesseth & saith that sundry times she hath seen & been almost killed by the Appartion of
John Willard
of Salem Village Husbandman on & before the 18. May. 1692 .
Abegall Williams
did deliver this testimony to us the Jurriars for Inqwist this 3'd of June: 1692. and did afarme to the truth of it
(Reverse)
Abig. Williams
ags't
John Willard
(MS Am 1147.3 Dept. of Rare Books and Manuscripts, Boston Public Library, Boston, MA. )
