SWP No. 22.12

(
Testimony of Samuel Webber
v.
George Burroughs
)
[August 2, 1692]
Samuel Webber
aged about 36 years Testifieth and sayth that aboute seaven or eight Yeares agoe I Lived at Casco Bay and
George Burroughs
was then Minester there, and haveing heard much of the  great strength of him s'd
Burroughs
; he Coming to our house wee  ware in discourse about the same and he then told mee that he had  put his fingers into the Bung of a Barrell of Malasses and lifted it  up, and carryed it Round him and sett it downe againe.
Salem  August 2'd 1692.
*Samuell Webber
Jurat in Curia
(Reverse)
Sam Webber
Cont:
Burroughs
( Essex County Court Archives, Salem - Witchcraft, vol. 2, no. 22. Massachusetts Supreme Judicial Court, Judicial Archives, Masssachusetts State Archives, MA. )
