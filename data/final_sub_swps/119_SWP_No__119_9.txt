SWP No. 119.9

(Deposition of
Sarah Coleman
Vs.
Margaret Scott
)
[September 15, 1692]
The Deposition of
Sarah Coalman
, who saith, about the fivetenth of August last past that she was tormented three or four times by
Marget Scot
, of Rowly, or her appearance, by pricking, pinching, and choaking of me, and I do verily believe that she is a witch.
Sarah Coleman affirmed
before te Grand Inquest, that the above written Evidence is truth, uppon her oath 15th September, 1692.
( Thomas Gage, The History of Rowley. (Boston: Ferdinand Andres, 1840) 174-175.
