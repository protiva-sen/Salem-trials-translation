SWP No. 65.3

(Deposition of
Ann Putnam, Jr.
v.
Elizabeth Hart
)
[+ May 16, 1692 ]
The Deposistion of
Ann putnam
who testifieth and saith that I have often seen the apperishtion of
gooddy heart
among the witches butt I did not know who she was: nor she did me no hurt tell the 13th of may 1692: that she came to my father house parsonally and tould me who she was and asked me if she had ever hurt me: but ever sence that day she has hurt me most greviously severall times and urgeth me greviously to writ in hir book
(Reverse)
Ann putnam
ag't
G. Hart
