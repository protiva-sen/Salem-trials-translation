The Juriors for our Sov'r Lord & Lady the King and Queen doe present That
Rebechah Jacobs
Wife of
Georg[e] Jacobs
of Salem Village --
In the County of Essex aforesaid Husbandman upon the  Eighteenth day of May --
In the yeare aforesaid and divers other days and times as well before as after Certaine detestable Arts called Witchcraft and Sorceries [Wicke]dly Mallitiously and felloniously hath used practi[ced an]d Exercised At and in Salem Village -- Aforesaid in upon and against one
Elizabeth Hobert
of Salem -- aforesaid Single Woman -- by which said Wicked Acts the Said
Eliz Hobart
the day & yeare aforesaid and divers other days and times as well before and after was and is Tortured Aflicted Consumed Pined Wasted and Tormented #[and also for Sundry other Acts of Witchcraft by (torn) said
Rebeckah Jacobs
-- Comitted and done before and Since that time] and against the peace of our Sov'r Lord & Lady theire Crowne and Dignity and the forme in the [Stattute] In that case made and Provided
Witnes
