SWP No. 12.2

( Testimony of
Joseph Fowler
Regarding
Sarah Bibber
, in Support of
Rebecca Nurse
)
[++ June 29, 1692]
The Testimony of
Joseph fowler
, who Testifieth that
Goodman Bibber
& his wife, Lived at my house, and I did observe and take  notice, that
Goodwife Bibber
was a woman, who was very idle in  her calling And very much given to tatling & tale Bareing makeing  mischeif amongst her neighbo'rs, & very much given to speak bad words and would call her husband bad names & was a woman of a  very turbulent unruly spirit
(Reverse) Against
Bibber
&
Wife
Joseph fowler
( Essex County Court Archives, Salem - Witchcraft, vol. 2, no. 120, Massachusetts Supreme Judicial Court, Judicial Archives,  Massachusetts Supreme Judicial Court, Judicial Archives, Massachusetts State Archives, Boston, MA )
