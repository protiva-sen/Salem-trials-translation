SWP No. 107.5

(Physical Examination of
John Proctor
and
John Willard
)
[June 2, 1692 ]
We whose names under written haveing searched the bodyes of
John procter sen'r
&
John Williard
now in the Goale & doe not find any thing to farther suspect them
Dated June 2, 1692
Rondel ap're testis
*John Rogers
*J. Barton
Chyrg'n
*Joshua Rea Jun'r
*Jno Gyles
*John Cooke
*William Hine
*Ezekiel Cheever
(Reverse) Return of
Doctor Barton
& other men that
Search't
Willard
&
Procter
( Essex County Court Archives, Salem -- Witchcraft Vol.1, no. 50, Massachusetts Supreme Judicial Court, Judicial Archives, Massachusetts State Archives, Boston, MA. )
