SWP No. 107.17

(Testimony of
Abigail Williams
v.
John Proctor
)
[May 31, 1692]
The Testimony of
Abigail Williams
+ witnesseth & saith that divers times in the month of April last past & particularly on the.4.6. eleven &.13. dayes of the same month, she the said
Abigail
hath been much vexed with the apparition of
John Proctor Senior
of Salem Husbandman, by which apparition she the said
Abigail
hath been often pinch't & otherwise tortured.
May 31. 1692. Attested before
(Reverse)
Abig: Williams
ags't
John Proctor Sen'r
( Essex County Court Archives, Salem -- Witchcraft Vol. 1, no. 54, Massachusetts Supreme Judicial Court, Judicial Archives, Massachusetts State Archives, Boston, MA. )
