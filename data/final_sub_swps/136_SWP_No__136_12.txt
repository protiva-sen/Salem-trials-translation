( Deposition of

v.

)
[+ July 2, 1692]
the deposition of
humpry Clark
aged about 21 yere saith that about a yere agoo I was asleep and about midnight the bed shook & I awaked and saw a woman stand by the bed side which when I well Looked semed to me to be
goodwif wills
which jumpid to the tother corner of the house & then I saw hir no more
Jurat in Curia
(Reverse)
Humphey Clerk.
