SWP No. 69.9

(Deposition of
Mercy Lewis
v.
Abigail Hobbs
)
[+ April 19, 1692]
The deposistion of
mircy lewes
agged about 19 years who testifieth that about the 17th of April 1692 I saw the Apperishtion of
Abigail Hoobs
the daughter of
william Hoobs
com and afflect me by pinching and almost choaking me urging me to writ in hir book and so she continewed hurting of me by times tell the 19th of April: being the day of hir examination but as soon as she began to confess she left ofe hurting me
(Reverse)
Mercy.Lewis
against
Abigall.Hobbs
( Essex County Court Archives, Salem -- Witchcraft Vol. 1, no. 160, Massachusetts Supreme Judicial Court, Judicial Archives, Massachusetts State Archives, Boston, MA. )
