SWP No. 137.33

( Testimony of
Benjamin Wilkins
&
John putnam
v.
John Willard
and
Sarah Buckley
)
[++ May 18, 1692]
The Testimony of
benjamin wilkns
aged about. 36: years saith That about the 12: of may last marcy lues being at my fathers hous tould us that she saw
john wilard
and goody bucly upon my father
wilkins
presing his belly and my father complained of extreme paine in his bely at the same time: then
John putnam
struck at the aperistions then
marcy luis
fel down & my father had ease emediatly:
John putnam
testifieth to the same above written
(Reverse)
Benj. Wilkins
Contra
Willard
( Essex County Court Archives, Salem -- Witchcraft Vol. 1, no. 257, Massachusetts Supreme Judicial Court, Judicial Archives, Massachusetts State Archives. Boston, MA. )
