SWP No. 37.5

(Deposition of
Mercy Lewis
v.
Giles Corey
)
[September 9, 1692 ]
The Deposistion of
Mercy lewes
agged about 19 years who testifieth and saith that on the 14'th April 1692 I saw the Apperishtion  of
Giles Cory
com and afflect me urging me to writ in his book and  so he contineued most dreadfully to hurt me by times beating me  & almost braking my back tell the day of his examination being the  19th April and then allso dureing the time of his examination he did  afflect and tortor me most greviously: and also several times sence  urging me vehemently to writ in his book and I veryly beleve in my  heart that
Giles Cory
is a dreadfull wizzard for sence he had ben in  prison he or his Apperance has com and most greviously tormented  me.
Mercy Lewis:
affirmd to the Jury of Inquest. that the above  written evidence: is the truth upon the oath: she has formerly taken  in the Court of Oyer & terminer: Sept'r 9: 1692
(Reverse)
Mercy Lewis
again't
Geoyles Cory
( Essex County Court Archives, Salem -- Witchcraft Vol. 2 no. 85. Massachusetts Supreme Judicial Court, Judicial Archives, Massachusetts State Archives, Boston. MA. )
