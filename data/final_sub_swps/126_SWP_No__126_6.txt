[Torn] The Jurors for our Sov'r Lord and Lady the King & Queen doe Present That
Job #[S] Tookey
of Beverly In the County of Essex Waterman The Seaventh day of June In the Yeare aforesaid and divers other days and Times as well before as after Certaine detestable Arts Called Witchcraft and Sorceris Wickedly Mallitiously and felloniously hath used practised and Exercised At and in the Towne of Salem in the County of Essex Aforesaid in upon and against one
Mary Warren
of Salem Aforesaid Single Woman by which said Wicked Acts the said
Mary Warren
the day & yeare Aforesaid and divers other days and times #[as] both before and after was and is Tortured Aflicted Consumed Pined Wasted and Tormented, and also for Sundry other Acts of Witchcraft by the said Job Stuky Committed and done before and Since that time against the Peace #(and also for Sunday other Acts of Witchcraft by the Said ^Job Stooky Robert) #(Susanah) S Shelden #(S?ford) Comitted and done before and Since that time against the peace) of our Sov'r Lord and Lady the King and Queen theire Crowne and Dignity and the forme of the Stattute In that case made and Provided
(Reverse) Ponet Se
Non Cul: (= not guilty)
