SWP No. 163.2

That my Lord President be pleased to acquaint his Ma'ty in Councill with the account received from New England from Sir
Wm. Phips
the Governor there touching Proceedings against severall persons for Witchcraft as appears by the Governor's letter concerning those matters.
(George Lincoln Burr, ed., Narratives of the Witchcraft Cases, 1648-1706  [New York, Charles Scribner's Sons, 1914], 196-198. The letter was addressed to William Blathwayt, clerk of the Privy Council, and it is he who added the memorandum.)
