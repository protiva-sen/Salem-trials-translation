SWP No. 169.23

(Return of the Town of Wenham)
[December 30, 1692]
At a metting of the inhabitants of Wenham this: 30'th of december: 1692:
Richard hutten
and
Samuell Kimball
ware Chosen to sarve upon grand jurye and James freind and
John abbye
on the jurye of tryalls as wittness my hand
*William faierfeild
Constable of wenham
( Mass. Archives Vol. 135 No. 96 )
