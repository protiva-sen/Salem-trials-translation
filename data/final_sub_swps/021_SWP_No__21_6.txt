SWP No. 21.6

(Deposition of
Elizabeth Hubbard
v.
Sarah Buckley
)
[September 14, 1692]
The Deposistion of
Eliz. Hubberd
who testifieth and saith that I  cannot say that ever
goody Buckly
hurt me but on the 18'th may 1692 being the day of the Exami'n of
Sarah Buckly
the wife of
william Buckly
I saw hir or Apperan[c]e torment and afflete
mary walcott
and
Ann putnam
#[& also severall times sence] & I do beleve: that the s'd
Buckly
is a witch: & afflicted the above named persons by witchcraft:
Eliz Hubbert:
owned to the grand Inquest that: the above written evidence is the truth: Sept'r 14 1692
Eliz: Hubbard
Jur in Cur[ia]
(Reverse) Eliz Hobert agst Sarah Buckley
( Massachusetts Archives Collection, vol. 135, no. 45. Massachusetts State Archives, Boston,  MA. )
