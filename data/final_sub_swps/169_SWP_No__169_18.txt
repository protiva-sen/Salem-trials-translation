SWP No. 169.18

(Return of the Town of Andover)
[December 30, 1692]
Andover: December 30'th 1692: In Obediance unto this Above Riten worant: I have Asembled the free holders & other the Inhabitance of Our Town Togither & thay Have Chosen #[Samuel]
Joseph Marble: Sene'r
&
henery holt
sene'r for the Jury of Tryals for the above mentioned Cort: & have Somonsed Them to apere acording to warant
As atest
*Ephraim foster
Constable of Andover
