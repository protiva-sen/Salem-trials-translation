SWP No. 45.9

( Statement of
John
and
Mary Arnold
for
Mary Esty
and
Sarah Cloyce
)
[++ September 9, 1692 ]
These may Cartify home it may Consarne that wee hous names are  underritten being dasired by sum of the Realeations of
Mary estwek
and
sarah Cloise
to give our obsarvation how they behaved tham sales  while thay Remained in Bostorn prison we dow affirme [th] at wee  [torn] saw noe ill Carreg or Behavor in tham But that thare daportment wase verey sobere and Civell as wittnes our hands
John Arnold
Marey Arnold
this is truee copie
( Essex County Court Archives, Salem -- Witchcraft Vol. 1, no. 296. Massachusetts Supreme Judicial Court, Judicial Archives, Massachusetts State Archives, Boston, MA. )
