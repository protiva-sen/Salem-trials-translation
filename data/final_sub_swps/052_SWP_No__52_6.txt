SWP No. 52.6

(Deposition of
Mary Walcott
v.
Abigail Faulkner Sr.
)
[September 17, 1692]
The deposistion of
who testifieth and saith that  about the 9.th August 1692 I was most dreadfully afflected by a  woman that tould me hir name was
abigail Falkner
.but on the 11th. of August being the day of the examination of
Abigail falkner
she  did most dreadfully afflect me during the time of hir examination  I saw
Abigail falkner
or hir Apperance most greviously afflet and  torment
sarah phelps
and
Ann putnam:
and I veryly beleve in my  heart that
Abigail Falkner
is a wicth and that she has often affleted  me and the afforesaid persons by acts of wicthcraft
The above name mary Walcutt affirmed before the Grand inquest  that the above written Evidence is truth upon her Oath 17: Sept.:  1692
(Reverse)
Mary Walcott
Depo Agst:
Aba: Falkner
Jurat in Curia
( Essex County Court Archives, Salem -- Witchcraft Vol. 2, no. 42. Massachusetts Supreme Judicial Court, Judicial Archives, Massachusetts State Archives. Boston, MA.  )
