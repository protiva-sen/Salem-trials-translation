SWP No. 63.25

(Deposition of
Sarah Bibber
v.
Sarah Good
)
[June 28, 1692 ]
The deposition of
Sarah Biber
aged 36 years testifieth and sayeth that the saterday night before
goode Dustin
of Reding was examined I saw the apparition of
Sarah goode
standing by my bedside, and shee pulled aside the curtain and turned down the sheet and Looked upon my child 4 years old and presently upon it the child was stracke into a great fit that my husband and I could hardly hold it
Sara biber
one her oath did owne this har testimony
before the Jurriars for Inqwest: this. 28: of June 1692
Jurat
Sarah Viber
(In margin)
G. Vibber
ag'st
Goody Good
(Writen on the reverse side there appears an itemized bill)
