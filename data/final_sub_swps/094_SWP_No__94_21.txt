SWP No. 94.21

( Deposition of
Johnnah Childin
v.
Rebecca Nurse
)
[++ June 3, 1692]
The deposision of
Johannah Childin
testieth and saieth that upon the: 2'd of June: 1692 that the aparition of goody nurs and
goodman Harrwood
did apeare to her and the said
Harrwood
did look goodey nuss in the face and said to her: that she did murder him by pushing him off the Cart and strock the breath out of his body.
( Essex County Court Archives, Salem -- Witchcraft Vol. 1, No. 76,  Massachusetts Supreme Judicial Court, Judicial Archives, Massachusetts State Archives. Boston, MA. )
